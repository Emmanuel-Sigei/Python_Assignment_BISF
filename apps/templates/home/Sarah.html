In the depths of my darkest moments, I'm Sarah, a survivor of gender-based violence. It all began with subtle control, but gradually, it escalated to physical and emotional abuse. I felt trapped in a never-ending nightmare. The isolation, the fear, the bruises I tried to hide—it was my life for too long.

One day, though, something inside me stirred. I found the strength to break free from the chains that bound me to my abuser. It was hard, incredibly hard. But with the support of some incredible friends and professional therapy, I began to rebuild my life, one piece at a time. The scars, both visible and invisible, started to heal.

Now, I'm an advocate for survivors. I work tirelessly to help others find their strength, just as I did. Together, we share our stories, lend a listening ear, and hold hands through the darkest hours. Because no one should ever have to endure what we did, and no one should have to face it alone.
